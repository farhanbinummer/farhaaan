Hoop Art

Hoop art, also known as embroidery hoop art or hoop embroidery, is a form of textile art that involves creating designs on fabric using a needle and thread, typically in a circular embroidery hoop. This art form has gained popularity in recent years due to its versatility and the endless possibilities it offers for creating unique and personalized pieces.

Frame work

Frame art refers to the process of framing artwork, photographs, or other objects to display and protect them. A well-chosen frame can enhance the beauty and impact of the artwork, as well as provide protection against damage from light, dust, and other environmental factors.

Save The Date Work

Save the date artwork is a form of design that is used to announce a special event, such as a wedding, anniversary, or other milestone celebration. The purpose of a save the date design is to give guests a heads up about the event and provide them with the necessary details to plan accordingly.
